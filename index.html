Roofing FAQ

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most single‑family projects take 1–3 days depending on size, layers, and weather. We'll provide a schedule up front.

Do you offer repairs or only full replacements?

We handle both. We can diagnose issues and recommend the most cost‑effective fix before suggesting replacement.

What roofing materials do you install?

Architectural asphalt shingles are most common. We also install metal accents, skylights, and gutters/guards.
